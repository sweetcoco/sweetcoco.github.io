Your first iOS app

Building apps for iDevices always starts with Xcode. Lets get through a few basic steps before we dig into writing any code:

  • You'll need a macbook
  • Upgrade to the latest OSX (Sierra as of this writing)
  • Download Xcode

Open Xcode and then follow these steps:

  • New Project
  • iOS
  • Single View Application
  • Product Name: Counter, Language: Swift, Devices: iPhone, uncheck all three checkboxes.
