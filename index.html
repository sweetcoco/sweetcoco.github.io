Relax

It's just code

Before I was a wildly talented and highly sought-after software engineer, I was a kid with a strong interest in writing code and a developing caffiene dependence. I spent hours scouring the internet for answers to every programming question under the sun. I was constantly met with old outdated answers, threads that were closed with "Nevermind! Just figured it out!" and didn't actually post what their solution was, and trouble with just knowing what to even google in general.

I'm here to help you. For Free.

Everything on this blog is free to read. I'll teach you how to code, I'll walk you through building your first app and way beyond. However, there are no comment sections on my posts, the comment thread for each post is on my Patreon. I always respond.

For the price of a coffee, you can become a patron.

Patrons are able to ask any question on my Patreon page and I will answer to the best of my abilities. I will even write sample code for you and hold your hand as much as possible. If you're not interested in asking questions but you still want to let me know I'm doing a good job, even a $1 pledge is highly appreciated, and even comes with a small perk!
